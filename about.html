Veema Events is a trusted importer and supplier of event and décor supplies serving Kenya and the wider East African region.

Our product range includes table linens, seat covers, charger plates, runners, chafing dishes, glassware, and a variety of event styling essentials designed to elevate event setups.

We are committed to providing products that combine durability, elegance, and affordability, helping event professionals deliver outstanding experiences while maintaining cost efficiency.
